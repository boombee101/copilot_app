Welcome to the TVA Copilot Help Center. This tool gives beginner friendly help for Word, Excel, Outlook, Teams, and PowerPoint. You can get clear manual steps or a clean Copilot prompt that you can paste directly.

About this tool

This assistant is designed for TVA employees who want quick, plain English help with Microsoft 365. For every task, you get two kinds of help:

  • Step by step manual instructions - simple directions so you can do it yourself.
  • A ready to use Copilot prompt - crafted to get high quality results inside your chosen Microsoft app.

Use either path or both. No technical jargon. No sensitive data.

How it works
  1. Pick your Microsoft app - Word, Excel, Outlook, Teams, or PowerPoint.
  2. Describe your goal or problem in plain language.
  3. You get two outputs:
    • Beginner friendly manual steps you can follow now
    • A ready to copy Copilot prompt tailored to your task
  4. Use your preferred path - follow the steps or paste the prompt into Copilot.
